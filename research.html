World Cup Data Project

For my Data Management, Estimating and Visualization class we were assigned a data visualization project where we combined two sets of data to tell an interesting data story. I chose to combine the highest paid athletes in each country with how many world cups each country has. For this project I had to collect and clean data, merge it in tableau, and design the visualization in tableau.

Travel Market Research

For my internship with Epic Travel I was tasked with providing extensive market research on a U.S. customer base. To do this I interviewed 12 different people who enjoy travel to understand their travel habits as well as their perceptions of different travel destinations.
